The Sargassum Problem

Massive sargassum buildup is affecting coastlines, marine life, and local economies. When it washes ashore, it damages beaches, produces strong odors, and discourages visitors.

Impact on Tourism

Clean beaches are essential for tourism. Sargassum threatens hotels, beach houses, jobs, and the income of coastal communities.
